This website was created as our SLX project to try and research a topic revolving around a UN sustainability goal. It is IMSA’s mission to try and help achieve these seventeen goals, and this study was made around the UN goal of “Zero Hunger”. The website was made and contributed to by Ethan Charoenpitaks, Jonathan Handjojo, Owen Larkin, and Nathan Vortia. Our information can be found at the top of the page under the “Pages” tab.
